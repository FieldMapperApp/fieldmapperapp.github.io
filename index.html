About

FieldMapper makes geo-referenced data collection easy. Just take a set of variables and customise FieldMapper's map interface according to your needs. Start surveying bounded systems or interviewing people now—because it matters where things happen!

Learn more about how FieldMapper works in the Documentation. FieldMapper is open-source—contributions are welcome!
